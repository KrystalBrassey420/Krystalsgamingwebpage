Weapons

Within in each weapon type there are trees these are the different varients of the weapons. Each weapon has a bone tree and a ore tree and with these trees its stems to different monsters you fight in the game. Not all of them contain all monster varient weapons.

The 14 Weapon Types

Name Description

Great Sword

Large and powerful but slow, this weapon is one of the hardest hitting weapons in the game. Dealing a large amount of damage per second. However this weapon is tricky to wield its slow and cumbersome nature makes it hard to land your hits, mastering this weapon is hard but not impossible. This weapon has the ability to sever tails and this allows you to carve the tail as a reward.

Sword And Shield

 Beginner friendly and mobile, this weapon is suited for those who are new to the game, it deals a moderate amount of damage and allows for a moderate defense. Although not to be underestimated, in the right hands this weapon is very powerful, much like the hunting horn

Dual Swords

 Light and extremely fast, this weapon is suited for those who prefer to evaded and attack quickly. However damage per hit is low but completeing a combo and dealig a large amount of hits allows for a large damage per second, rivalling that of the great sword.

Long Sword

 Nimble and moderately powerful suited for those prefer precision based weapons. With a long range and varied combos, the long sword is difficult to master and more of an advanced weapon type.

Hammer

 This is a slow weapon but not as slow as the great sword, this weapon is suited for those who want to deal a large amount of damage as well as stun the monster. Its is great for breaking parts off the monsterincreasing your rewards dropped. This is my favourite weapon.

Hunting Horn

 Long ranged support weapon, this is the slowest weapon in th game, this weapon does damage whilst creating a wonderful melody, the different combos have different melodies, and each melody has a buff attached to it. This weapon is hard to master but in capable hands can deal a great deal of damage. This weapon can also provide buffs for your whole team whilst playing multiplayer, hence its suitability for support based combat.

Lance

 Strong defense and offense, this is a long ranged weapon great for taking a large amount of damage, this weapon is suited for multiplayer and team strategy based combat. This is a tank type weapon, using it to take a large amount of damage in team combat will allow the members to deal a large amount of damage without taking any. However its damage is substancial and will allow you to hold your own in combat

Gunlance

 High offense but limited range, this weapon much like the lance, suited for tank type combat, taking lots of damage in team combat, this weapon is also able to deal a substancial amount of damage and powerful in the right hands

Switch Axe

 Fast, versatile and deals elemental damage. This fast weapon allows you to switch between close ranged and long ranged combat, the Switch Axe sticks true to its name as it switchs between an axe and a sword, the axe being close range and the sword being long range. This weapons combos allows you to deal a substancial amount of elemental damage and normal damage.

Charge Blade

 Versatile, very powerful and fast although not as fast as the Dual Blades. This weapon switches between Sword and Shield and an Axe. This weapon is considered overpowered and is very technical and one of the most difficult to master.

Insect Glaive

 High mobility coupled with aerial attacks and buffs, this weapon uses the glaive to deal attack damage and the kinsect to collect extract from the monster to aquirebuffs. It has a wide range of attacks mostly focused on aerial based combat.

Bow

 Fast,medium ranged and suited for status effects. This weapon is perfect for those who prefer a ranged weapon type, it embues status effects quickly making it easier to deal damage.

Light Bowgun

 Very mobile and rapid firing, the light bowgun is perfect for the quick shooting players, who want to get in deal as much damage as they can and get out relativly unscathed, with a medium to long range depending on your ammo type.

Heavy Bowgun

 Slow, short ranged powerful attacks, this is another tank type weapon, which can deal a lot of damage whilst taking a large amount of damage as well
