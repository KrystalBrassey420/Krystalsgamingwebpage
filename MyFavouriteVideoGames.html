Monster Hunter Wold:Iceborne is not a typical game, the games objective is to hunt monsters as the name suggests, there are different monster types and there are there different difficuly ranges. In order to hunt these monsters you have what is called scount flies, they help track the monsters, the more tracks and other monster evidance you collect the quicker you will be able to find the monster at the start of quests. Two which are the base game Monster Hunster World and one which is Iceborne Together they complete the game Monster Hunter World:Iceborne

The ranges of difficulty are:

Low Rank(Monster Hunter Hunter World)

This ranges from 1 Star to 5 Stars
Within theses lets call them stages you will recieve quests to hunt different types of monsters,these monsters do become increaseingly harder to fight as you progress through the quests

High Rank(Monster Hunter Hunter World)

This ranges from 6 stars to 9 Stars Here you will fight mostly the same monsters but they are now hard to fight. The difficulty of the monsters icrease substancially. In these stages they introduce a few new monsters to the game aswell.

Master Rank (Iceborne)

Master rank is an expansion of the game which comes from Iceborne. The Stages range from 1 Star to 6 Stars, in the expansion they introduce new monsters as well as variant monsters, that are significantly harder to fight.

The 14 Weapon Types

Name Description
Great Sword Large and powerful but slow, this weapon is one of the hardest hitting weapons in the game. Dealing a large amount of damage per second. However this weapon is tricky to wield its slow and cumbersome nature makes it hard to land your hits, mastering this weapon is hard but not impossible. This weapon has the ability to sever tails and this allows you to crave the tail as a reward.
Sword And Shield Beginner friendly and mobile, this weapon is suited for those who are new to the game, it deals a moderate amount of damage and allows for a moderate defense. Although not to be underestimated, in the right hands this weapon is very powerful, much like the hunting horn
Dual Swords Light and extremely fast, this weapon is suited for those who prefer to evaded and attack quickly. However damage per hit is low but completeing a combo and dealig a large amount of hits allows for a large damage per second, rivalling that of the great sword.
Long Sword Nimble and moderately powerful suited for those prefer precision based weapons. With a long range and varied combos, the long sword is difficult to master and more of an advanced weapon type.
Hammer This is a slow weapon but not as slow as the great sword, this weapon is suited for those who want to deal a large amount of damage as well as stun the monster. Its is great for breaking parts off the monster increasing your rewards dropped. This is my favourite weapon.
Hunting Horn Long ranged support weapon, this is the slowest weapon in th game, this weapon does damage whilst creating a wonderful melody, the different combos have different melodies, and each melody has a buff attached to it. This weapon is hard to master but in capable hands can deal a great deal of damage. This weapon can also provide buffs for your whole team whilst playing multiplayer, hence its suitability for support based combat.
Lance Strong defense and offense, this is a long ranged weapon great for taking a large amount of damage, this weapon is suited for multiplayer and team strategy based combat. This is a tank type weapon, using it to take a large amount of damage in team combat will allow the members to deal a large amount of damage without taking any. However its damage is substancial and will allow you to hold your own in combat
Gunlance High offense but limited range, this weapon much like the lance, suited for tank type combat, taking lots of damage in team combat, this weapon is also able to deal a substancial amount of damage and powerful in the right hands
Switch Axe Fast, versatile and deals elemental damage. This fast weapon allows you to switch between close ranged and long ranged combat, the Switch Axe sticks true to its name as it switchs between an axe and a sword, the axe being close range and the sword being long range. This weapons combos allows you to deal a substancial amount of elemental damage and normal damage.
Charge Blade Versatile, very powerful and fast although not as fast as the Dual Blades. This weapon switches between Sword and Shield and an Axe. This weapon is considered overpowered and is very technical and one of the most difficult to master.
Insect Glaive High mobility coupled with aerial attacks and buffs, this weapon uses the glaive to deal attack damage and the kinsect to collect extract from the monster to aquire buffs. It has a wide range of attacks mostly focused on aerial based combat.
Bow Fast,medium ranged and suited for status effects. This weapon is perfect for those who prefer a ranged weapon type, it embues status effects quickly making it easier to deal damage.
Light Bowgun Very mobile and rapid firing, the light bowgun is perfect for the quick shooting players, who want to get in deal as much damage as they can and get out relativly unscathed, with a medium to long range depending on your ammo type.
Heavy Bowgun Slow, short ranged powerful attacks, this is another tank type weapon, which can deal a lot of damage whilst taking a large amount of damage as well
