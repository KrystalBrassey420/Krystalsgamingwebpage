This Is A Guide to the Game

Monster Hunter World:Iceborne

Monster Hunter World:Iceborne is not a typical game, the games objective is to hunt monsters- with the help of your palico buddy-as the name suggests, there are different monster types and there are three different difficuly ranges. In order to hunt these monsters you have what is called scount flies, they help track the monsters, the more tracks and other monster evidance you collect the quicker you will be able to find the monster at the start of quests. There are two difficulties which are part of the base game Monster Hunter World and one which is Iceborne together they complete the game Monster Hunter World:Iceborne

Palicoes, Grimlekyne and Tail Raiders

The Palicoes are a cat-like companion which accompanies you on your missions and assist you in hunting the monster and gather items and reward with in the loacale and quest. You only have one palico per character, that you are able to customize and name this is your only palico. Through out your quests you will come accross palicoes and Grimlekine your palico will be able to communicate them and they will join you on your quests.

Grimlekyne, Gajalaka and Boa Boa

Grimlekyne are tricky, in order to gain them as a tailraider you must complete certain quests, there are 4 types of Grimlekine and two other tailraider companions known as Gajalaka and Boa Boa. Gaining these tail raiders trust will allow you to use the other gadgets and communicate with some of the small monsters such as: Jagras, Girros, Kestedon, Gastedon and Wulg

Gadgets

There are 7 Palico Gadets

  1. Vigorwasp

    2. The palico pulls out a vigorwasp and rides it to the hunter, once he reaches you the vigorwasp explodes with a healing spray. When leveled you the palico can place a vigorwasp station, like one found in the locales.
    In Iceborne

  2. Flashfly Cage

    3. The palico places a cage with a flashfly inside it, the hunter is then able to run to it, slash it and blind the monser if timed correctly. When leveled the Palico can deploy and thunderbug trap, this shocks the monster like a shock trap, but if overused the monster can become resistant.
    In Iceborne you are able to unlock the Boombug cage, when the palico places it you are able to lure the monster into it making it explode damaging the monsters.

  3. Shieldspire

    4. The palico deploys a sheild with a face on it,they use it to taunt the monster into attacking it and using the shield to stay alive. The next ability is shieldbash which can stagger and stun the monster if it makes contact.
    In Iceborne

  4. Coral Orchestra

    5. The palico pulls out a large coral instrument, it plays one of several songs that applies buffs such as Attack up, Defense up, Bleeding Resistance, Stamina Drain Down. When leveled up the Palico gains access to a second instrument with some improved buffs, such as the ability to negate enviromental damage from lava, giving the hunter the earplugs effect, divine protection or stun resistance/negation.
    In Iceborne

  5. Plunderblade

    6. The palico uses a Plunderblade to snatch items from the monsters(Like scales, fangs claws.etc) items gathered this way go directly to the palicoes inventory and will be recieved after the quest is completed. When the Plunderblade is leveled up the Palico is able to throw the blade, if it hits the monster,it will drop an item which may be a talon or scale, or slinger ammo such as piercing pods.
    In Iceborne

  6. Meowlotov Cocktail

    7. The Palico pulls out a ceramic pot and throws it at the monster causing it to explode and damage the monster. When upgraded you Palico is able to use a small canon and shield this can easily stagger large monster.

  7. Tailraider Signal


The Ranges Of Difficulty Are:

Low Rank(Monster Hunter Hunter World)

This ranges from 1 Star to 5 Stars
Within theses lets call them stages you will recieve quests to hunt different types of monsters,these monsters do become increaseingly harder to fight as you progress through the quests

High Rank(Monster Hunter Hunter World)

This ranges from 6 stars to 9 Stars
Here you will fight mostly the same monsters but they are now hard to fight. The difficulty of the monsters icrease substancially. In these stages they introduce a few new monsters to the game aswell.

Master Rank(Iceborne)

Master rank is an expansion of the game which comes from Iceborne. The Stages range from 1 Star to 6 Stars, in the expansion they introduce new monsters as well as variant monsters, that are significantly harder to fight.
